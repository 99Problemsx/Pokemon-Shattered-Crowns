## Overview

**Pokémon Shattered Crowns** is an epic fan-made Pokémon adventure featuring an original story spanning **55 chapters**, **4 perspective interludes**, and over **700 cutscenes**. Experience a mature, emotionally-driven narrative that explores themes of friendship, sacrifice, love, loss, and the power of hope.

## Story Structure

### Act 1: Awakening
*Chapters 1-7*
> A young trainer begins their journey, unaware of the ancient power awakening within them. Strange marks appear, guardians stir, and destiny calls.

### Act 2: Rising Shadows
*Chapters 8-19 + Perspective Interludes*
> The Chosen discover their purpose as darkness spreads across Verdania. Alliances form, enemies emerge, and the stakes grow higher with each battle.

### Act 3: The Gathering Storm
*Chapters 20-28*
> All pieces fall into place. The final confrontation approaches as heroes and villains clash in an epic struggle for the world's fate.

### Act 4: Shattered Crowns
*Chapters 29-35*
> The culmination of everything. Nidhoggr rises. Heroes fall. Sacrifices are made. And the world hangs in the balance.

### Act 5: The Eternal Seal
*Chapters 36-55*
> One year later, the seal weakens. A journey to the forgotten region of Astoria. The Crown of Unity. The final, eternal battle. And a TRUE ending.
