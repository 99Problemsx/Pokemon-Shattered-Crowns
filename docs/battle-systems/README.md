# ⚔️ Battle Systems

Plugins that enhance, expand, or completely rework the battle experience — from dynamic music shifts to persistent rival trainers that evolve based on your defeats.

| Plugin | Version | Description |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| [Battle Arena](battle-arena.md) | 1.0.0 | Post-game challenge tower with themed floors, streaks, and rewards |
| [Battle Rewards Ex](battle-rewards-ex.md) | 1.0.0 | Ranks battles S/A/B/C based on performance, awards bonus items |
| [Companion Battles](companion-battles.md) | 1.0.0 | Tag-team battles with story companions using custom AI |
| [Dynamic Music](dynamic-music.md) | 1.0.0 | Context-aware BGM: HP-based, boss phases, day/night, weather |
| [Nemesis System](nemesis-system.md) | 1.0.0 | Rival trainers that remember your defeats, grow stronger, and hunt you down |
| [Trainer Rematch](trainer-rematch.md) | 1.0.0 | Trainers become re-battleable with scaled teams after story progress |

---

## Recommended Combos

- **Nemesis System + Battle Rewards Ex** — Nemesis trainers that drop better loot at higher ranks
- **Dynamic Music + Companion Battles** — Phase music shifts during tag-team boss fights
- **Battle Arena + Trainer Rematch** — Re-battle old rivals in the arena with scaled teams
