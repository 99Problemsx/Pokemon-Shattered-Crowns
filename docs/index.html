The Story

A journey that will change everything

You are not ordinary.

A strange mark appears on your skin. Visions haunt your dreams. And an ancient power stirs within you.

You are one of three Chosen—mortals bound to legendary guardians tasked with protecting the world from an ancient evil sealed away long ago.

Together with Lyra, a kind-hearted healer bound to Xerneas, and Kael, a troubled young man with a dark past bound to Yveltal, you must uncover the truth behind the Shattered Crowns and stop the primordial beast Nidhoggr from consuming all existence.

But the path is not easy. Allies will fall. Enemies may become friends. And you will have to decide what—and who—you are willing to sacrifice to save everything.

Features

What makes this journey unique

Complete Story

55 chapters across 5 acts with a fully realized beginning, middle, and end. Over 19,000 lines of story content.

Deep Characters

Characters that grow, change, and face real consequences. Relationships that matter. Deaths that mean something.

Two Regions

Explore Verdania, your home region, and journey to the forgotten land of Astoria in Act 5.

Legendary Partners

Zacian, Xerneas, Yveltal as guardian companions. Giratina and Arceus join the final battle.

Your Choices Matter

Dialogue choices affect relationships and scenes. Multiple paths to key moments.

Quality of Life

Modern Pokemon Essentials features, speed up option, improved UI, and quest tracking.

The Chosen

Three heroes bound by destiny

The Player

Guardian: Zacian

A young trainer from Willowbrook who becomes the leader of the Chosen. The weight of the world falls on your shoulders—but you never carry it alone.

Lyra

Guardian: Xerneas

The heart of the group. Kind, compassionate, and stronger than she knows. Her healing abilities save lives throughout the journey.

Kael

Guardian: Yveltal

The shadow. A young man haunted by his past. Reluctant to trust, slow to open up, but fiercely loyal once he does.
