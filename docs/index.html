The Beginning

Your Destiny Awaits

A journey that will change everything you know

You are not ordinary.

A strange mark appears on your skin. Visions haunt your dreams. And an ancient power stirs within you.

You are one of three Chosen—mortals bound to legendary guardians tasked with protecting the world from an ancient evil sealed away long ago.

Together with Lyra, a kind-hearted healer bound to Xerneas, and Kael, a troubled young man with a dark past bound to Yveltal, you must uncover the truth behind the Shattered Crowns.

The Threat

Nidhoggr

The primordial beast of destruction threatens to consume all existence. Only the Chosen can stop it.

"Allies will fall. Enemies may become friends. And you will have to decide what—and who—you are willing to sacrifice to save everything."

Game Features

What makes this journey unique

Complete Story

55 chapters across 5 acts with a fully realized beginning, middle, and end. Over 19,000 lines of story content with meaningful character arcs and emotional moments.

19,000+ lines 700+ cutscenes 4 interludes

Deep Characters

Characters that grow, change, and face real consequences. Relationships that matter. Deaths that mean something.

Two Regions

Explore Verdania, your home region with forests and cities, then journey to the forgotten land of Astoria in Act 5.

Legendary Partners

Zacian, Xerneas, Yveltal as guardian companions. Giratina and Arceus join the climactic battle.

Meaningful Choices

Your dialogue choices affect relationships and scenes. Multiple paths to key moments shape your journey.

Quality of Life

Modern Pokemon Essentials features, speed up option, improved UI, and quest tracking system.
